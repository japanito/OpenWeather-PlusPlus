Weather Station Build Notes

DS1820 ADDRESS: 0x10,0x0F,0x43,0x5B,0x02,0x08,0x00,0x0D
DS2438 ADDRESS: 0x26,0x8A,0x9F,0x21,0x01,0x00,0x00,0xC8

const uint8_t readHumidity = 0x00;
const uint8_t readSolar = 0x09;

ds2438->writeSetup(readHumidity); //change to read HIH3610 or whichever humidity sensor you are using
ds2438->writeSetup(readSolar); //change to read Clarion CLD240 photodiode
